# AgroShield

##  Problem Statement

Many farmers do not have timely access to agricultural experts, due to which crop diseases and pest attacks are often identified very late. This causes heavy damage to crops and financial losses. Our project aims to help farmers detect crop problems early and provide suitable guidance.

---

## About the Project

AgroShield is a web-based application developed for our hackathon. The main objective of this project is to assist farmers in identifying crop diseases and pest attacks by simply uploading an image of the affected crop.

The application analyzes the uploaded image and provides information about the possible disease or pest along with its symptoms, causes, preventive measures, and treatment suggestions. We also aim to make the platform simple enough for farmers to use without requiring technical knowledge.

---

## Objectives

- Detect crop diseases at an early stage.
- Help farmers identify pest attacks.
- Provide simple and understandable treatment suggestions.
- Reduce crop losses by promoting early action.
- Make agricultural knowledge easily accessible.
