# AgroShield

## Problem Statement

Many farmers do not have timely access to agricultural experts, due to which crop diseases and pest attacks are often identified very late. This causes heavy damage to crops and financial losses.

AgroShield aims to help farmers detect crop problems early and provide suitable, understandable guidance.

---

## About the Project

AgroShield is a multilingual web-based crop health assistant developed for our hackathon. It helps farmers identify possible crop diseases and pest attacks using crop information, reported symptoms, uploaded images, and voice input.

The platform will provide probable disease information, prevention and treatment guidance, crop-health history, seasonal alerts, and access to agricultural experts or nearby support services.

---

## Key Features

- Multi-language support
- Farmer location, terrain, climate, and crop information
- Crop calendar with Rabi, Kharif, and Zaid season identification
- Soil-health information
- Crop health history
- Pest and disease-season alerts
- Symptom reporting with photo upload and voice input
- Possible disease and pest identification
- Treatment and prevention recommendations
- Nearby medicine shops, agricultural experts, and government centres
- Community discussion space for farmers
- Feedback collection and fact verification

---

## Objectives

- Detect crop diseases at an early stage.
- Help farmers identify pest attacks.
- Provide simple and understandable treatment suggestions.
- Reduce crop losses by promoting early action.
- Make agricultural knowledge easily accessible.
